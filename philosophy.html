Philosophy

Records come before AI.

NoveraFlow is shaped around the belief that a diary should first be a place to leave a trace, not a place to be judged.

Small records matter

A sentence, a symbol, or a quiet blank can still become part of a personal flow. The service should make returning easy, not heavy.

Feedback should not decide

AI feedback in NoveraFlow is observational. It can point to a recurring theme, but it should not diagnose, prescribe, or close the meaning too quickly.

Symbols stay open

Symbols are not rewards or personality types. They are gentle shapes that grow beside records and leave interpretation to the person who keeps them.

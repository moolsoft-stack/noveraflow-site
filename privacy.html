Privacy Policy

NoveraFlow is in MVP development. This page states the privacy direction that will guide the service before public operation.

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Data we expect to handle

NoveraFlow may handle diary records, selected symbols, app settings, and reflection signals needed to provide the diary experience. The MVP should collect only what is needed to operate and improve the product.

Diary records

Diary records are personal. NoveraFlow is designed so reflection and AI feedback remain close to the user's record, without turning private writing into public display by default.

AI processing

When AI features are used, records may be processed to generate gentle feedback or identify recurring patterns. NoveraFlow should avoid medical, diagnostic, or prescriptive interpretation.

Public beta update

Before public beta or production launch, this policy should be reviewed with the actual data flow, storage period, deletion process, subprocessors, and contact channel.

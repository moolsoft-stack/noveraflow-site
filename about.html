About NoveraFlow

NoveraFlow is a quiet AI diary for people who want to keep small daily records without turning life into a score.

A diary for living patterns

NoveraFlow helps short records gather into a gentle sense of flow. It is built around writing with ease, noticing recurring thoughts, and keeping reflection close to everyday life.

What NoveraFlow avoids

NoveraFlow does not diagnose, rank, or force conclusions. AI feedback is designed to reflect patterns carefully, leaving room for the person who wrote the record.

MVP status

NoveraFlow is currently in MVP development. The first version focuses on the diary experience, symbol direction, and soft feedback.
